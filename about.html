About Us

Ziyakha Engineering is a black youth female-owned company committed to providing first-rate safety solutions to meet the specific needs of mining and construction. We deliver exceptional safety solutions customised to address varied requirements, enhancing workplace safety, training, and compliance.

Vision

"Our aspiration is to be among the principal stakeholders who spearhead the initiative of Safety and innovation across Africa while ensuring a safe working environment for our teams, clients and communities."

Mission Statement

"We are committed to delivering high-quality Safety training that meets the evolving needs of our clients and communities while prioritizing environmental stewardship and social responsibility. By leveraging local resources, fostering partnerships, and investing in our workforce, we aim to enhance safety across the continent, driving economic progress and improving the quality of life for all Africans."

Value Statement

  • Safety First: We prioritize the health and safety of employees, clients, and communities.
  • Quality and Reliability: We ensure our manuals and training materials meet international standards.
  • Community Empowerment: We uplift local communities by creating jobs, providing training, and investing in local economies.

Strategic Objectives

  • Safety: Provide ongoing training and implement rigorous health and safety protocols.
  • Quality Management Compliance: Stay compliant with local, national, and international standards.
  • Product Diversification: Develop and introduce products and services to meet emerging market demands.
  • Strategic Partnerships: Collaborate with contractors, architects, investors, and builders to enhance market presence.
  • Market Expansion: Expand to new geographical markets and regions.
