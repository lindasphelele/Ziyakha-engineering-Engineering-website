About Us

Welcome to ZIYAKHA ENGINEERING, your premier partner in construction engineering consultancy and safety training based in South Africa. We are committed to excellence in high-risk environments, ensuring that your projects are both safe and successful.

Our mission is to empower businesses with the knowledge and solutions they need to operate seamlessly and efficiently. With a team of seasoned experts, we combine extensive industry insights with innovative strategies, enabling your operations to not only meet compliance standards but also excel in safety and productivity.
